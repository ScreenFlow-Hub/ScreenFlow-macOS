## Why ScreenFlow is the #1 choice for Mac creators

- 4K/8K recording with perfect Retina quality  
- Simultaneous screen + camera + mic + mic  
- Industry-best editing timeline (nested clips, markers, multi-track)  
- Animated titles, callouts, annotations, mouse effects  
- Direct upload to YouTube, Vimeo, Google Drive  
- Apple Silicon native (M1–M4) – blazing fast exports  

This community toolkit gives you **thousands of ready-to-drop assets** used by top YouTubers and course creators.

## What's inside (instant pro videos)

- `Templates/` — YouTube intros, course lessons, software tutorials, reviews  
- `Animated Titles/` — lower-thirds, kinetic typography, big titles  
- `Callouts & Annotations/` — arrows, zoom boxes, highlights, mouse clicks  
- `Transitions/` — smooth zoom, glitch, film burn, light leaks  
- `Stock Media Packs/` — background music, sound FX, motion backgrounds  
- `LUTs & Color Grades/` — cinematic, teal-orange, vintage, clean looks  
- `Export Presets/` — YouTube 4K, TikTok vertical, LinkedIn, fast render  
- `Overlays/` — device frames, countdown timers, subscribe animations

## Installation (macOS) – 60 seconds

1. Click the button →downloads  
2. Drag folders into ScreenFlow’s Media Library  
3. Restart ScreenFlow → everything appears instantly
